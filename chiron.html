Bugatti Chiron Super Sport

Top speed

Bugatti Chiron Super Sport it was the fastest car in 2021. It became the first series-production car to break the magical 300-miles-per-hour barrier. Achieving a speed of exactly 304.773 mph (495 km/h), it was the fastest production hyper sports car ever.

Bugatti Chiron Pure Sport Engine

The Power Of The Engine

All Chiron models are motivated by an 8.0-liter 16-cylinder powerplant. This beast of an engine employs four turbochargers to generate a mighty 1500 horsepower and 1180 pound-feet of torque. Bugatti claims that the Chiron makes the dash from zero to 60 mph in a mere 2.3 seconds, and it has a top speed of 261 mph.
